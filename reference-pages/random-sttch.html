RANDOM-STTCH

[GENERIC-FUNCTION]
OMLindberg
Function Reference
ARGUMENTS:
  - n n-chords [default = 8]
  - low lowest-note [default = 2100]
  - high highest-note [default = 10800]

Description:

Generates 'n' random symmetrical twelve-tone chords. The ambitus of all chords is defined by low (lowest note) and high (highest note).
One of the 15 Lindberg's hexachords is randomly selected for each chord.
